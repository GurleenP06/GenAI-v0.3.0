Usage Agreement

Legal guidelines US and Non-US Person users must follow

All large language models are susceptible to giving inaccurate or inappropriate responses and may not represent RTX's views or values. Evaluate your received responses against your contextual knowledge, RTX's Core Values, and specifically your knowledge of RTX's Global Corporate Policies (GCP), Functional Corporate Policies (FCP) and GCP-60 "Responsible Artificial Intelligence". OSKAR is meant to be a helpful resource, not a definitive source for information. Consult your Legal or HR resources for further clarification on your specific needs.

RTX will retain logs of your inputs to evaluate performance and security.

DO NOT input any of the following categories of information into OSKAR:

  • Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) / Covered Defense Information (CDI)
  • Sensitive Personal Information (Business Contact Information is permissible)
  • Technical data
  • Third-party information
  • Data or information that is controlled by a non-U.S. government or jurisdiction, for example, UK Strategic Military Information, EU Restricted Information, or Chinese Important Data

DO NOT copy and paste documents. Inputs are limited to 4,096 characters or less. Character limitation imposed to restrict potential for transferring technical data/content appropriate for the OSKAR environment.

AI-generated content or code CANNOT be used or incorporated into RTX products or services without prior approval. This includes externally facing documents or documents that may be revealed to third parties as well as any proposals, deliverables, products, services, or documents that become part of RTX command media, corporate policies, or standard work. RTX knowledge topics are limited by non-technical data content appropriate for the OSKAR environment.
